How It Works

Install. Chat. Done.

1

Get from Mac App Store

One click to download. The AI model is bundled inside the app. No extra setup required.

2

Open and start chatting

Launch the app. Type your question. Responses stream in real-time, powered by your own hardware.

3

Your data stays yours

Every conversation is stored locally in SwiftData. No cloud sync. No accounts. No one else can see it.

What we DON'T do

  • ✕ Connect to the internet
  • ✕ Require accounts or sign-ups
  • ✕ Send telemetry or analytics
  • ✕ Include crash reporting SDKs
  • ✕ Phone home for license checks
  • ✕ Train on your conversations
  • ✕ Share data with anyone
  • ✕ Include WebKit or web views
