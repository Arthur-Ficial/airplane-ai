1. OVERVIEW

Airplane AI is an offline-only AI assistant for macOS. It runs entirely on
your device. This privacy policy explains what data the app handles, where
it goes (nowhere), and what controls you have.

The short version: your data never leaves your Mac. There is nothing else
to say, but regulations require us to say it at length.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

2. DATA COLLECTION

Airplane AI collects no data. Zero. None.

- No analytics or usage tracking.
- No telemetry of any kind.
- No crash reporting.
- No device fingerprinting.
- No advertising identifiers.
- No cookies, beacons, or pixels.

The app has zero network entitlements. The macOS App Sandbox enforces this
at the operating system level — the app is physically unable to connect to
the internet, send packets, or resolve DNS. This is not a policy promise;
it is a technical constraint enforced by Apple's sandbox.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

3. ACCOUNTS AND AUTHENTICATION

Airplane AI does not require or support:

- User accounts
- Sign-up or registration
- Login or authentication
- Email verification
- Any form of identity

You open the app and use it. That is all.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

4. LOCAL DATA STORAGE

Your conversations are stored locally in the app's sandboxed container on
your Mac. This data is:

- Readable only by Airplane AI and your macOS user account.
- Not synced to iCloud or any cloud service.
- Not shared with any other application.
- Not backed up to any remote server.

You can delete all conversation data from Settings > Danger Zone within
the app. Uninstalling the app also removes all stored data.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

5. AI MODEL

Airplane AI bundles a Gemma language model (by Google) that runs locally
via the llama.cpp inference engine. The model:

- Is included in the app bundle at install time.
- Runs entirely on your Apple Silicon GPU and CPU.
- Never communicates with any server.
- Never sends or receives data over any network.

All AI inference happens on-device. Your prompts and the model's responses
never leave your machine.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

6. IMAGE ANALYSIS

When you attach an image, Airplane AI uses Apple's on-device Vision
framework to extract text and labels:

- VNRecognizeTextRequest for OCR and handwriting recognition.
- VNClassifyImageRequest for image classification labels.
- VNDetectDocumentSegmentationRequest for document detection.

All Vision processing runs on-device using Apple's built-in machine
learning models. No image data is sent to any server — Apple's Vision
framework operates entirely locally.

The extracted text is shown to you before it is sent to the AI model.
The model sees only the extracted text, never the raw image.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

7. SPEECH RECOGNITION

When you use voice input, Airplane AI uses Apple's SFSpeechRecognizer.
When Apple provides on-device recognition for the selected language,
Airplane AI asks for it. Some languages may rely on Apple's standard
speech-recognition path instead.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

8. AI OUTPUT DISCLAIMER

AI-generated responses may contain errors, inaccuracies, or misleading
information. You are responsible for independently verifying any important
information provided by the AI. The developer makes no guarantees about
the accuracy, completeness, or reliability of AI outputs.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

9. THIRD-PARTY SOFTWARE

Airplane AI uses the following open-source components:

- llama.cpp — inference engine (MIT License)
- Gemma model — AI model (Apache 2.0 / Google Gemma Terms of Use)

No third-party analytics SDKs, crash reporters, ad networks, WebKit
views, or network libraries are included.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

10. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Airplane AI does not collect data from anyone, including children. Because
no data is collected or transmitted, there are no special provisions
needed for children's privacy. The app contains AI-generated content that
may not always be appropriate for all ages; parental discretion is advised.

This privacy policy may be updated when new versions of the app are

policy within the app after updates.
